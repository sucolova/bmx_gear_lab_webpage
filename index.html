Products

Network-Frame

One of the most progressive BMX frames we’ve ever made, Dan Kruk’s signature Network Frame has been tested in the lab and in the streets for a solid year to prove it can handle the technical ferocity and all-out war Dan put’s his bike through. Dan wanted a shorter 12.5” chain stay and updated geo from the Battleship Frame, making the Network one of the most responsive and agile BMX frames to date. Utilizing our new invest casted yoke for improved stiffness and tire/sprocket clearance, the Network Frame really is a frame from the future. Includes our lifetime guarantee.

Pathfinder

Felix Prangenberg’s signature Pathfinder Frame is a product which reflects the originality, progression and power of Felix’s riding. Boasting a shorter 12.75” chainstay and a taller 9.35” standover height, the Pathfinder Frame utilizes ground-breaking offset thickness chain stay and bottom bracket shell, to strengthen the frame in areas susceptible to grind damage. With fresh colourways and a set of graphics which were developed closely with Felix, the Pathfinder Frame is a future Wethepeople classic. Includes lifetime guarantee.

Battleship

Combining cutting edge hydroforming technology with input from the Wethepeople team, the Battleship Frame is the perfect match for the progressive street rider. Boasting an incredibly short 12.7" chainstay, thicker cnc machined dropouts (designed to work flawlessly with hub-guards and pegs) ; the Battleship is a super tough, no-nonsense street weapon with our most responsive geometry yet. Includes lifetime guarantee.

Hybrid Freecoaster Rear Wheel

We took our game-changing Hybrid Freecoaster and laced it up to our Logic Rim creating one hell of a wheel. Fully convertible from Freecoaster to Cassette, giving you two wheels in one.

Message Fork

The Message Fork is a super tough, yet elegantly designed fork for park riders looking for the ultimate balance between strength and weight. Using a super tapered leg, shorter 28mm offset and a unique invest cast fork cap, the message fork comes with a re-enforced longer steerer tube allowing you to run a gyro or your personal choice of spacers and dial in your bike to perfection. Includes lifetime guarantee.
